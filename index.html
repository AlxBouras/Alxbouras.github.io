Biography

I am currently an MSc student at Université Laval (Quebec, Canada). My research project centers on the recommendation of publications, specifically addressing multimodal regression involving text and images in a highly imbalanced input space, with a particular focus on recommendation algorithms for news articles on social media.

My research interests span deep learning, reinforcement learning, and natural language processing.
